FAQ

What is Elijah Kitaka and what does it offer?
Elijah Kitaka is a soulful Afro-soul artist from Uganda, signed to Swangz Avenue. His music blends African rhythms with heartfelt lyrics and captivating melodies, bringing a unique sound to the contemporary African music scene.
Who can join the Elijah Kitaka community?
Anyone who is passionate about music and the arts can join the Elijah Kitaka community.
How do I join the Elijah Kitaka community?
You can join the Elijah Kitaka community by signing up for a membership on the website.
Are there any membership fees to join the community?
Yes, there is a membership fee to join the Elijah Kitaka community. The fee varies depending on the membership plan you choose. There is also a free version.
How do I access the creative-led courses on the website?
You can access the creative-led courses by logging into your account and browsing the course catalog.
What types of courses are available on Elijah Kitaka?
Elijah Kitaka offers a wide variety of courses on topics such as music business, music production, songwriting, vocal technique, instrument mastery, and personal development.
How do I get started with the creator tools available on the website?
You can get started with the CreatorSPC tools by logging into your account and accessing the tools section of the website.
Can I sell my art or music on the Elijah Kitaka platform?
No, we do not have a selling platform on our platform at this time.
How can I connect with other artists in the community?
You can connect with other artists in the community by joining groups, attending events, and collaborating on projects.
What types of events does Elijah Kitaka organize?
Elijah Kitaka organizes events such as live concerts, workshops, and masterclasses.
How can I participate in the events organized by Elijah Kitaka?
You can participate in the events organized by Elijah Kitaka by registering on the website or attending in-person if the event is held locally.
Can I submit my own event to be featured on the website?
No, but you can post your events, music, and other announcements in our community channel “Plug Yourself.”
How can I stay updated with the latest news and updates from Elijah Kitaka?
You can stay updated with the latest news and updates by subscribing to the Elijah Kitaka newsletter, following their social media accounts, or checking the website regularly.
What are the benefits of joining the Elijah Kitaka community?
The benefits of joining the Elijah Kitaka community include access to creative-led courses, creator tools, a platform to connect with other artists, and opportunities for collaboration and networking.
How can I contribute to the community as a member?
You can contribute to the community by sharing your knowledge and expertise, participating in events and projects, and supporting other members.
Is there a support team available to assist me with any issues or questions?
Yes, there is a support team available to assist you with any issues or questions you may have. You can contact them through the website or email [email protected]
Can I access Elijah Kitaka on my mobile device?
Yes, our website, community, and courses are all built with mobile-friendly designs.
How do I cancel my membership with Elijah Kitaka?
You can cancel your membership through the settings page on your CreatorSPC dashboard.
How can I provide feedback or suggestions to the Elijah Kitaka team?
You can provide feedback in our “Ideas & Feedback” community channel, or by emailing [email protected]
